What is Bancannabis?


Growing Community

Bancannabis is the digital community of cannabis, we seek the integration of all participants in the productive cycle of cannabis through innovative and decentralized solutions.


Building and decentralized ecosystem

Bancannabis is creating the most advanced supply chain in cannabis markets through blockchain solutions and IOT integrations we provide to any one in the word the opportunity to be part of this industry.


Eco-conscious and Innovative focused

Our mission is to create the biggest community of innovation and development in cannabis and decentralized solutions related with their supply chain.
